Kaitlyn Martin

Hey all, it’s me Kaitlyn! Hair is my passion and my paws have been in it since my teens. My friends always seemed to return home after a sleepover with new hair color and cuts - which made me very popular with my friends (and less so with their parents haha). It was no surprise I soon became a receptionist at Bliss Salon and Spa, a local salon in my hometown of Lansing, MI. The owner, Dallas, opened my eyes to the opportunities the industry has to offer. It was there that I started a two year apprenticeship to eventually become a licensed cosmetologist. Dallas and the rest of the team instilled confidence in me and my skills, as well as the importance of education and creative freedom. They taught me in a hands-on environment and I learned how to be a great colorist specializing in blonding, balayage, and beautiful finishing techniques. The impact of working for such a supportive team was huge, and as much as I cherished my time there, getting out of small-town Michigan became a huge goal for me. Dallas helped me achieve that goal and move to Chicago to become a big-city stylist.

Hello Chicago! Sure enough I made it to this beautiful city. Logan Square was my first neighborhood, and I made a home at Logan Parlor for a year and a half. There my cutting skills blossomed, it finally clicked and cutting hair became more natural no matter the length. It was at Logan parlor that my passion to give back and create events for the community started. I started coordinating events to support the LGBTQ chi community. And that's when I learned another side of the business, and to give back to Chicago’s community. I enjoyed my time in Logan Square, But I was ready for a switch up of neighborhoods in July 2020.

I took a leap over to wicker park. It is there my journey begins as a solo stylist. I am happy to provide a private, comfortable setting for us to reach your hair goals. The city unlocked more style and confidence within myself, and that ultimately helps me in finding the right look for you as well. Beauty is rooted from within and is in the eyes of the beholder, together we can create a look that embodies the most confident you.
